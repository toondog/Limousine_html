Skyline Limo and Sedan Service is your premier luxury limousine company

which serves the Los Angeles, Orange, Riverside, San Bernardino and Ventura counties.

Skyline Limo and Sedan Service is where you’ll find your luxury car or limo and with experienced chauffeurs and the most competitive pricing in town you’ll soon find out why we’re the number one choice for limo transportation and executive travel. So if you are looking for a Platinum Style time we invite you to come experience the difference.
